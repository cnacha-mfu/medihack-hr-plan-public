# Work-Hour Plan — June–September 2026

## Scope
- **3 groups × 5 students = 15 students**
- **280 hr per student** across the whole project
- Planning window: **June – September 2026**

## Constraints
| Month | Rule |
|-------|------|
| June | Free — any day, no monthly cap |
| July | Free — any day, no monthly cap |
| August | **Weekends only**, **≤ 60 hr/month** |
| September | **Weekends only**, **≤ 60 hr/month** |

Because Aug + Sep are capped at 60 hr each (120 hr max combined), **June + July must cover at least 160 hr** of every student's 280.

## Recommended per-student allocation
| Month | Hours | Notes |
|-------|------:|-------|
| June | 80 | Free month |
| July | 80 | Free month |
| August | 60 | Weekends only (at cap) |
| September | 60 | Weekends only (at cap) |
| **Total** | **280** | ✓ |

> June–July split is flexible (any combo summing to 160). If the mid-June project start makes June tight, shift to **June 60 / July 100**. Aug and Sep should stay at the 60 hr cap since they are the constrained months.

## Fixed work-day schedule (identical for all 15 students)
All students work the **same dates and times**. Free months use full **8 hr weekdays (08:00–17:00, 1 hr lunch)**; the weekend-only months use **7.5 hr weekend days (09:00–17:00, 30 min lunch)**.
